Cherry Pie

This is an easy cherry pie recipe
using canned cherries!


Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh, sour cherries or three (14.5 oz) cans sour cherries
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamond
  • Pie crust (homemade or store bought)
  • 1 large egg white, beaten with a fork
  • granulated sugar to sprinkle on top

Steps

Preparing the channed cherries

  1. Drain the juice from the cans of cherries into a saucepan.
    Reserve 1/3 cup in a small bowl and add in cornstarch and stir well to combine.
    Set the cherries aside.
  2. Heat the saucepan with the juice in it over medium heat. Add sugar and stir well.
    Bring mixture to a low boil. Stir in the dissolved cornstarch mixture and cook until thickened.
    Takes about 2-3 minutes. Stir in butter and lemon juice. Fold in the cherries.
  3. Let the mixture cool down to room temperature

Pie filling

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Put your pie crust in your desired form. Make whatever shape and aesthetic you prefer.
  3. Put the cherries filling in your pie crust
  4. Brush a thin layer of eggwash on your pie crust and sprinkle som sugar on top
  5. Bake the pie at 400 degrees F for about 40-45 minutes.
    Cover the pie with a piece of tinfoil after 25 minutes.
  6. Remove to a wiring cooling rack and let it cool for serveral hours.
  7. Enjoy!
