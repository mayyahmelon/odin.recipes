Cheesecake


Homepage

This is a cheesecake recipe that has been passed down by the women in my family.
I am sharing this today as it is one of my life motives that sharing is, indeed, caring.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon brown sugar
  • 7 Tablespoons butter

Cheesecake filling

  • 32 oz cream cheese (softened to room temperature)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ⅔ cups sour cream
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature, lightly beaten)

Steps

Preparing the crust

  1. Preheat oven to 325F (160C).
  2. Prepare Graham Cracker crust first by combining graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and brown sugar, and stirring well. Add melted butter and use a fork to combine ingredients well.
  3. Pour crumbs into a 9” Springform pan and press firmly into the bottom and up the sides of your pan. Set aside.

Making the filling

  1. Add cream cheese in a bowl and stir until smooth and creamy
  2. Add sugar and stir again until creamy
  3. Add in sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Stir until well-combined.
  4. While stirring the mixture on a low speed, gradually add the lightly beaten eggs, one at a time. Make sure all the ingredients are well combined.
  5. Pour the cheesecake batter into the prepared springform pan.
  6. Bake the cheesecake in the center of your oven at 325F (160C) for about 75 minutes. The cheesecake should have a light golden brown edges with the center being jiggly.
  7. Remove from the oven and let the cheesecake cool for around 10 minutes.
  8. Use a knife to gently loosen the crust from the springform.
  9. Let the cheesecake cool for another 1.2 hours before transferring to the fridge. Let the cheesecake cool atleast 6 hours, preferably overnight.
  10. Enjoy!
