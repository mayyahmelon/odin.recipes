Strawberry Milkshake


Homepage

This is the perfect milkshake to sit at the balcony in hot summer days with. It's refreshing and yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 10 scoops(or 2 cups) Strawberry ice cream of your choice
  • Toppings of your choice, for example, whipped cream and fresh cut strawberries

Steps

  1. Let your ice cream temper for about 5-10 minutes at room temperature. Chill your milkshake glass in the freezer
  2. Combine the milk, heavy cream, and ice cream in the jar of a high speed blender.
  3. Blend on low and work your way up to medium, until your desired milkshake consistency. Dont forget to scrape down the sides once or twice to get everything incorperated.
  4. Taste it, add more milk if you want it thinner, add more ice cream if you want it thicker.
  5. Pour the milkshake in the chilled glass and decorate with your favorite toppings!
  6. Enjoy!
